# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of **laravel‑email‑blocker** pledge to create a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment‑free community for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio‑economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We commit to making participation in this project and its online spaces a positive experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Be courteous, respectful, and considerate in all interactions  
- Use welcoming and inclusive language  
- Be open to constructive feedback and differing viewpoints  
- Respect the time and effort of other contributors  
- Help maintain a community environment free of harassment and discrimination

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include (but are not limited to):

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary behavior of any kind  
- Personal attacks, abusive comments, or insults  
- Threats, stalking, or intimidation  
- Derogatory jokes or offensive comments  
- Sharing private or personal information without consent  
- Repeated disruptive behavior in project spaces

## Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by emailing to `sagautam5@gmail.com`

Provide as much detail and context as possible — all reports will be treated with confidentiality and seriousness.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action in response to Code of Conduct violations. This can include:

- A warning or explanation of the violation  
- Temporary or permanent removal from project communication channels  
- Blocking or removal of contributions if necessary

The maintainers’ decisions are final and aim to protect the community’s well‑being.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by this project, including but not limited to:

- GitHub issues and pull requests  
- Public discussions (e.g., Discussions tab, comments)  
- Emails and social media interactions related to this project
